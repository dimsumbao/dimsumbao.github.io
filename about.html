About Us

Kama'aina Krate was founded on two basic beliefs: that Hawai'i creates some of the most amazing, unique treats and that everyone could use a little bit more aloha spirit.

We also give 10% of our profits to helping Hawaii-based non-profit organizations. We want to help provide as many opportunities for the people of Hawai'i as possible.

Kama'aina Krate was the brain child of four Willamette undergraduate roommates. All four of these lovely ladies are from the island of Oahu and have lived there their entire lives, save for the last four years!

Jacqueline Arakaki

With a Psychology degree, she currently persues an MBA in marketing. In the mean time, she manages the website for Kama'aina Krate as well as all the marketing and social networking.

Torri Yoshizawa

With her degree in Enviornmental Science, she keeps in close contact with local businesses may it be designers, producers, or farmers. She is the company's buyer and keeps on top of the latest Hawaiian trends.

Megan Oshiro

With her degree in Rhetoric, she helps Torri with researching and buying the perfect products for each box. Also with her superb book keeping skills, she handles and mangages all the orders that come in.

Kacey Ota

With a Sociology degree in one hand and personability in the other, she sets out to handle the customer service aspects. She needs to stay on top of her game when she juggles all the customer service requests and emails.

Kama'aina Krate provides only made-in-Hawai'i products in every box. Each package is packed by hand with aloha. Every Krate is a little slice of the islands.
