Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Kama'aina Krate?

A Kama'aina Krate is a box that is mailed once a month with exclusive made in Hawai'i products. The products can be anything from snacks, accessories, clothing, hygiene products, and innovative gadgets!

These are things that you cannot find outside Hawai'i - none of that Hawaiian Host chocolate!

How long will it take to ship?

US Orders please allow for 1 week for shipping.

International Orders please allow for 3 weeks for shipping.

Can I replace (item x) with (item y)?

If you have any medical conditions such as allegies, please note them in the field available when you check out. We will be more than happy to substitute any products with healthy, safe alternatives.

Due to the way we order products for our box, we cannot give multiples of an item.

How do I update my billing information?

You can do so on the Checkout page.

If you need assistance you can shoot us an email or call us at 1-808-555-2275.

Where is my confirmation/tracking email?

The email that we keep in contact with is the email that you have your Paypal is attached to. Make sure to check your Paypal-linked email before contacting us.

Also check your Spam folder if possible. Sometimes our emails get filtered into the spam filter.

If you have checked all these places and still can't find our email, please feel free to contact us.

Kama'aina Krate provides only made-in-Hawai'i products in every box. Each package is packed by hand with aloha. Every Krate is a little slice of the islands.
