FAQ

How long will it take to ship?

Regular US Orders please allow for 1-2 weeks for shipping.

Rush US Orders please allow for 1-2 business days.

International Orders please allow for 3-4 weeks for shipping.

Rush International Orders please allow for 3-5 business days.

Will you make more, new Gentlemon?

Maybe! If you want to know about our new, work-in-progress, or other Gentlemon-related news:

  • Watch or visit Pluffers or Nyte on DA.
  • Sign up to be on our emailing list where we send out monthly newsletters about all our new products. Just email dangodewdrop@gmail.com and request to be on our mailing list!
  • Check our front page where we update our news!

I just saw Gentlemon being used for [insert place here]. Is that you guys?

We do not allow others to use our designs (Gentlemon and anything else here). If you see our designs being used on shirts, hats, or even as charms and being sold on a different site, please report them and inform us.
