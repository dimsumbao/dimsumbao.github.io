HOW IT WORKS

1. Choose a Package

2. Choose a Subscription

3. Enjoy the Aina

When you sign up for the Kama'aina Krate, every month you will receive a box in the mail with five (5) or more hand picked products for you and your loved ones - anything from snacks, accessories, clothing, hygiene products, and innovative gadgets! These could range from chocolate covered macadamia nuts or an aloha shirt or a solar powered watch!

If you or your family and friends crave more authentic tastes and feels from the tropical islands, we'll give you exclusive opportunities to order more products at an unbelievable rate.

10% of the profits of each box go to a different Hawai'i-based non-profit organization to help aid the people of Hawai'i. To learn more about each program and organization, click the sponser link for the month.

We have a package for everyone!

  • Hoaloha Package - For friends, co-workers, and anyone who needs a unique and exotic thank-you present! Includes products meant for a single person, such as shirts, a box of candy, and accessories.
  • 'Ohana Package - For family and loved ones who you want to share a little more of the aloha spirit with! Includes products meant to share such as large boxes of chocolates, bottle of wine, and jars of snacks.
  • Care Package - For those who are off at college, on trips, or just need a little something to remind them of home! Includes products meant for locals, such as cans of spam, li hing crack seed snacks, and kakimochi.
